Privacy Policy for SLEDDVR

Last Updated: August 11, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SLEDDVR (“we,” “our,” or “us”) handles information when you use our racing game. By playing SLEDDVR, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect:

  • Username – provided by you for the purpose of displaying on global leaderboards.

No other personal information (such as email address, real name, or contact details) is collected.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your username is used solely to:

  • Display your achievements on the SLEDDVR leaderboards.
  • Credit your performance in races and track best times for each map.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Your username and leaderboard records are stored using Unity Gaming Services, specifically Cloud Code and Cloud Save.

Data is retained for as long as possible but may be deleted when leaderboards are refreshed during major updates.

5. Data Deletion Requests

If you wish to have your username and leaderboard records deleted, please email your username (with exact spelling and capitalization) to:

exlgamingdev@gmail.com

We will process deletion requests as soon as reasonably possible.

6. No Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third parties.

7. Security

While no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, we take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at:
