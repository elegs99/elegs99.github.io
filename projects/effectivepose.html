EffectivePose: Real-time Pose Tracking

EffectivePose is an Android app that uses ML Kit to convert live video streams from a smartphone into accurate body pose estimations displayed in real-time.

Key features include GPU processing for reduced estimation times, achieving up to 60 fps on high-end devices.
