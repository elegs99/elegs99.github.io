Jedi Droid Attack transports players to a distant galaxy where they harness the Force and duel wield lightsabers against Sith droids. My role was instrumental in developing the intuitive force grab controller script, a cornerstone of gameplay, enabling players to interact with their surroundings like a Jedi.

This project highlights my expertise in virtual reality development, user interface design, and environmental storytelling, showcasing my ability to build interactive and engaging gameplay experiences in the rapidly evolving field of VR.
