SLEDDVR

❯

Solo-developed physics based sledding VR game for Meta Quest platform.

SLEDDVR is an immersive VR game featuring innovative movement mechanics and procedural terrain generation.

I independently developed and released SLEDDVR, implementing custom systems using Unity and C#. I optimized backend services with Unity Gaming Services for cloud-based leaderboards and created all 3D models, particle effects, and marketing assets. My efforts led to over 950 downloads in the first month, demonstrating my ability to deliver a full-cycle game development project that resonates with players.
